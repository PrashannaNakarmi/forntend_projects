welcome to our restaurant

It is simple to get to Cherry Bomb, a restaurant and café in Kathmandu, Bagmati Province. The structure was constructed in the past. The majority of the bricks contain inscriptions indicating that they were manufactured about 1903. The building's warm and inviting atmosphere has been carefully preserved throughout the refurbishment process. And that's what you'll discover here—true warmth and friendliness to put you at ease.
